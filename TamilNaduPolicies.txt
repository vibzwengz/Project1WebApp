<h3>Tamil Nadu</h3>
<ol>
<li>Like other states, TN also aims to attract huge investment for the EV industry in the state. The state has set a goal of INR 500 Bn in investment in EV manufacturing and created a comprehensive EV ecosystem in the state and thereby targetting the creation of 1.5 Lakh new jobs.</li>
<li>The Policy aims to:
<ul>
<li>Create robust infrastructure for electric vehicles including adequate power supply and network of charging points with favourable power tariff.</li>
<li>Promote innovation in EV for automotive and shared mobility by providing the ecosystem and infrastructure to make Tamil Nadu, the EV hub of India.</li>
<li>Create a pool of skilled workforce for the EV industry through the technical institutions available in the State and create new jobs in the EV industry.</li>
<li>Make Tamil Nadu the preferred destination for electric vehicles and component manufacturing units including battery and charging infrastructure.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
